Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how StopHand (“the App”) handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and this app is designed to work without collecting personal data.

1. Information Collection

StopHand does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All habits and notes entered in the app are stored locally on your device only.

2. Use of Information

Since no personal data is collected, there is no use, processing, or analysis of user information by the developer or any third party.

3. Data Sharing

StopHand does not share any data with third parties. No analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are used in the app.

4. Data Security

All information created within the app remains on your device and is protected by your device’s built-in security features such as passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID (if enabled).

5. Children’s Privacy

StopHand does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
